About Your Project

This Customer Prediction Project leverages machine learning models to predict customer behavior, preferences, and future purchasing patterns. By using historical data and customer interactions, businesses can make better-informed decisions to improve customer satisfaction and increase revenue.

Our goal is to help businesses build predictive models that can forecast customer behavior with high accuracy, enabling them to tailor marketing campaigns, offers, and customer engagement strategies effectively.
